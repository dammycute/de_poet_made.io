He Is Willing To Help

This is nothing but Delusion!

With what I've seen, here is my conclusion.

The world today is swimming in a sea of endless illusion.

And what can I say, this in turn brings a lots of confusion.

I am particular about you, You had a strong knowledge of what you are created for.

But what happened? Do you suddenly got bored?

You had a dream, You had a vision.

You had a goal, You had a mission.

But the fear, the guilt, resentment with you.

Blocked every possibilities of your dream.

You now prefer sitting at the corner of your bed watching film.

I can feel it, maybe it not that you've forgotten,

Maybe it just that you can't seems to bypass the hurtings.

Hey! Do you know He understands more than you do.

Do you know He was tempted like you too

Maybe the greatest consolation is in Heb 4:15-16



At the end, maybe it's not really about you

Maybe it's about myself

The feeling of a strong sense of purpose but a controversial thoughts and emotion within.

But in the real sense I realise: He Is Willing To Help





Fear, Guilt of our past mistakes, resentment, needs for approval and a lots of things controls our lives, they are those delusions stopping us from becoming and pursuing our God given dreams and visions.

What is stopping you from pursuing your God given dream.... (.......) You can fill the gap but do you know God understands what and why you are facing that problem...?

Do you know He wants you to fulfill His purpose more than you do...? No matter what is happening in your life He is able to Help.
