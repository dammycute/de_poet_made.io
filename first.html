Questions About Feelings

Puzzled with a question in my mind

Pondering on them everyday as if they are hard to find

Sincerity is all I want....

Are feelings real? Or are they some mental illusion

Which causes confusion?

Are they some part of emotion which must be sorted out...?

Or are they some daydreaming stuff which I must doubt...?

Because hatred and Love are some sort of emotions which comes a feelings.

How can a young man be helped, who can not understand the meanings (really confused whether to allow some feelings or not.)

Because some feelings seems to tamper with sanity.

Some feeling even hamper sanctity.

Why are feelings and emotions seemingly stronger than humanity...?

And we can only get help from divinity...



What should I do with my feelings throw them out or sort them out....?
