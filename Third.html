You Are Not Alone

Living in an age Where there is utmost uncertainty.

It is easier for a young man to lose his identity.

Surrounded with more than enough things to worry about.

It leaves us with a lots of Confusion, anxiety and doubt.

My main target is Confusion

Because he watches his victims suffer crazy conditions

It almost like you are going crazy.

It affects the dilligent and even the lazy.

Yea am aware about the normality

But am talking about the abnormality

When you are really confused with everything in your life.

A situation where you have a lots of decisions to make and you don't even know where to start.

Or a situation where all your beliefs and perceptions are becoming untrue.

And you are already looking like a fool.

A situation when you are bombarded with all sorts of informations

And you don't even know where to start your own calculations

A situation when nobody understands you anymore because you are acting strange.

But you also cannot explain the reasons behind your actions because you feel caged.

You are stucked between many opinions and you don't know what to say.

You look through the mirror and flipped through the page.

Searching and thinking you've arrived until everything begins to fade

At that point, all you could say is why.

Because it's gotten to a point when all you could do is cry.

You've been crippled to stay on your bed.

By the yes in your mind and a lots of no in your head.

You are really not alone!



God is willing to help if you allow him to...
